About

The Annual Techfest of Shri Shankaracharya Engineering college offers the chance of showing your talent. The Blind Coding is one of the way of showing your coding skills in which the code you write will be blur on the screen. It tests your coding as well as your typing skills. There will be no boundatin of using any specific pragramming language. You can choose language as per your own comfort.

So Hurry Up!!!!

Participate and Show us What You can do!!!

Time-Line

Registration

The Registration money is Rs.30. Payment can be done offline after registration.

First Round Starts

The First Round will start from 11 Feb to 15 Feb. Each day a problem will be updated on the site which the participants have to solve. A Winner will be chosen from this round.

Second Round

The competition will be held in the computer lab of SSEC on 16 Feb 2018. The Winner & Runner up will be chosen from this round.

Perks

Exciting prizes for Winners & certificates for all participants

The Prizes worth Rs.1000, Rs.800 & Rs.500 will be given to the Winners and the Runner up. The participation certificate issued by SSTC will be given to all the participants.
